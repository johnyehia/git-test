Caesar Salad

Some facts

Caesar salad is a popular and refreshing salad that can be enjoyed as a side dish or a main course. The combination of crispy romaine lettuce, tangy dressing, and crunchy croutons makes it a classic favorite.

Ingredients

  • 1 head of romaine lettuce
  • 1 cup croutons
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Wash and dry the romaine lettuce. Tear the leaves into bite-sized pieces and place them in a large salad bowl.
  2. Add the croutons and grated Parmesan cheese to the bowl with the lettuce.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the lemon juice, minced garlic, Dijon mustard, olive oil, salt, and pepper until well combined.
  4. Pour the dressing over the lettuce mixture and toss gently to coat the leaves evenly.
  5. Let the salad sit for a few minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  6. Serve the Caesar salad immediately as a side dish or add grilled chicken or shrimp to make it a complete meal.
